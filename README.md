# Options Strategy: Bullish Risk Reversal Strategy Analysis

This project analyzes various scenarios for a Bullish Risk Reversal strategy that involves selling a 1 Month 95% OTM put on the SPDR S&P 500 ETF (SPY) and buying a 105% OTM call. The strategy is evaluated as of April 09, 2025, following a strong intraday market rally of approximately 10%, one of the largest single-day moves in S&P 500 history.
